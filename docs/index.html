About me

I honestly do not know how to introduce myself in my own website without sounding pretentious. Anyway, this is a comfy place to explain myself and my research in a more personal way. As someone once said in Catalan, "passi, passi, que veurà el piset".

The person beyond the research

What is my research exactly?

I study neutrinos. A lot has been written about why neutrinos are a window to new physics, all the experiments which are going to understand them better, how are we going to use them for astrophysics and where to look for their masses in the Universe.
But me? I study them 'cause they're pretty damn fun.

My research on neutrinology

Breakin' the bubble: outreach

Science must not stay in academia. I have engaged a lot in outreach as a way of building bridges between physics and society, and as a way of promoting science in my mother language. Specially in the cringiest of ways: recording videos and podcasts for disseminating through all social networks.
